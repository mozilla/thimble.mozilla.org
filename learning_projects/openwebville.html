Welcome to

Open Webville

Open WebVille isn’t just your run-of-the-mill "Ville." You see, it’s managed by Madame Lille, a former french politician and robot who is relaunching her career in a new direction: focusing more on velvet bird painting, organic farming, and protecting the Open Web. In this series of mini-games Madame Lille has started putting together a guidebook for the citizens of Open WebVille to understand how to use and reference openly licensed works.

Use your Open Web ways and HTML dexterity to help her finish her work. These projects are offered in partnership with the School of Open.

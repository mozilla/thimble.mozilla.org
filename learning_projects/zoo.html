Welcome to the

Awesome Animal Builder

Create you own unique, weird and totally bizarre Awesome Animal!

We've drawn some of the world's most Evolutionarily Distinct and Globally Endangered animals (EDGE species) for you to remix and hack together to create your own awesome animals!

EDGE species often have amazing adaptations such as the ability to inject venom through their teeth or to roll up into a ball of yellow spikes! Have a go at building your own awesome animals by mixing adaptations together.

Don't forget to name and describe your new creations for all our friends and family to see.

This was made by the Zoological Society of London (ZSL). Learn more about their work conserving EDGE animals at EDGE of Existence.
